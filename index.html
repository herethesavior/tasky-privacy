Tasky for Android

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Tasky does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

All data you create in Tasky — tasks, notes, subtasks, categories, reminders, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device and is never sent to any server, third party, or developer.

2. Permissions We Request

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)

Used exclusively for the voice input feature, which lets you add tasks by speaking. Audio is processed on-device by Android's built-in speech recognition. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted by Tasky.

Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)

Used to deliver task reminders you set yourself. Notifications are scheduled and displayed entirely on-device. No notification content is sent to any external service.

Storage / File Access

Used only when you explicitly trigger the Export feature to save a backup file to a folder you choose on your device. Tasky does not access any other files on your device.

3. Data Storage

  • All task data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data persists across app updates but is deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Tasky has no account system, no login, and no cloud sync.
  • The app works fully offline and requires no internet connection.

4. Third-Party Services

Tasky does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services.

The voice input feature uses Android's on-device speech recognition, a system-level service provided by Android/Google. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how Android speech recognition handles audio on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Tasky does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

6. Data Security

Since all data stays on your device, its security is determined by your device's own security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
